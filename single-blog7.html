WHAT DOES IT MEAN TO BE A TEACHER IN SUCRE?

  • Date
  • January, 18th

Being a teacher is an exercise in planning, time and resource management. In our department you can find a variety of contexts in which the teacher must make use of his creativity and sometimes work even with his nails to provide a meaningful class for his students.

Being a teacher in Sucre means being able to visualize and understand the needs of students according to the context presented in class, It is to witness the diversity present in each classroom in which we work and work for the good development and growth of the entities involved. It is also to make use of ingenuity and creativity to promote activities that are beneficial without forgetting the conditions of their environment.

This personally represents a great challenge in my academic future, because one would like to think that thanks to your training academy is prepared to face every situation in the educational field, but that is not the reality, that is why for me to be a teacher, especially in this context, is to be able to adapt and use circumstances to create new material.

I hope to use my sensitivity as a tool to empathize with my students, and creativity to make the most of whatever I find useful and relevant to my class. I also aspire to use my critical-reflective thinking to help form autonomous citizens with social responsibility.
